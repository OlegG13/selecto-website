Sprout there

Loyalty application that
rewards you

Overview
Do you like to share regardless if that is your time or just a cup of coffee? The loyalty of our client brought him to us with the idea of creating an app that rewards people for their support of local businesses and kindness to the others. The more you shop and share the more valubale the rewards become. We loved the idea behind this app and did our best to make it come true
  • Industries Small Business, Ecommerce
  • Period June 2016 — October 2016
  • Services mobile app development,
    mobile and website design
  • Platforms iOS, Android, website
Design Process
The application consists of user and business part. Each of them follows certain goals, so at first, we had to build scenarios for each part in order to move on to creating wireframes and detailed designs
Flows structure
Together with our development team we put down all the actions that users and businesses should be able to do and organized them into a mind map to understand where they intersect and how we need to build their mutual interaction
User part
  • Creating user profile
  • Sprout map
  • Contacts and rewards
Business part
  • Creating business profile
  • Performance analytics
  • Rewards and QR code scanner
Wireframes first
The next step was to present the wireframes to our client in order to show our vision of the app design and hear George's point of view. Once everyone was happy with the wireframes, we moved on to creating actual user interface

Landing page
Landing page generalized the info for businesses and customers. With its look, we had had to attract new clients and instill the reliability. We succeeded by combining warm green and cozy brown colors, enrolling effects and brisk conclusions presented in every info section
